Frequently asked questions

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE HAVE ANSWERS.

⏱ How long does upholstery take to dry after cleaning?

Usually 12–24 hours at room temperature. In summer or with good ventilation, it can be less. We recommend opening windows and not using the sofa until it is completely dry.

🧹 Do I need to vacuum the sofa or carpet before cleaning?

Yes, thorough vacuuming is essential. Without it, optimal results cannot be achieved. When cleaning on-site, we can do this step together.

🗺️ Do you travel to my area?

We operate within approx. 25 km of Návsí – Jablunkov, Trinec, Mosty u Jablunkova, Hrádek, Bystřice and surroundings. Travel is free up to 15 km; above 15 km we charge 15 Kč/km.

⚠️ What if a stain cannot be fully removed?

Old or chemically fixed stains cannot always be removed 100%. We always inform you about realistic expectations upfront. The fresher the stain, the better the result.

💳 How can I pay?

We accept cash or bank transfer (QR code). Payment is due after the service is completed or when the machine is handed over.

🔄 How quickly can I get an appointment?

We usually respond within 24 hours and arrange a date for the nearest available day. We also work evenings after 5 PM and weekends by prior arrangement.
